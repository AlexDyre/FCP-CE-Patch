[h1]Description[/h1]
Patches the Fallout Collaboration Project to work with Combat Extended.

Most items still need balancing.
Even though I've done my best to test everything, there may still be errors, so feel free to let me know if you encounter any.

[b]Please report any bugs or errors/wrong stats in the discussions section![/b]

[b]Latest Changes[/b]
Added: Patches for Insitute and Railroad Teaser
Added: Patches Stupid Sexy Deathclaws and Enclave Armory (thanks to FluffyTowels)
Fixed: Apparel patch for US Army Equipment Teaser (class name changed)
Fixed: Missing patches for added Power Armor (T60 and Raider)
Fixed: Errors generated when certain mods were not loaded
